Ingredients:

- iPad, iPhone, or iPod touch
- 2 cups vocabulary
- 1 brown onion, coarsely chopped
- Salt, to taste


Preparation:

Mix all ingredients gently. Then, choose letters from the bowl to form words. Tap the SUBMIT button to eat the word. If you mess up, shake your spoon to put the letters back into the bowl.
When you have eaten enough, the waiter will bring you another bowl of soup.

WARNING: Be careful not to let soup get cold. If the soup stays out too long, the waiter may take your soup away from you.
